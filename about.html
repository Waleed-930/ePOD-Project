About the ePOD Project

The Electronic Proof of Delivery (ePOD) system is a course project for CPIS-358: Internet Application and Web Programming. It simulates how companies can move from paper-based proof-of-delivery to a digital platform using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Project Objectives

  • Digitize and simplify the delivery confirmation process.
  • Reduce paperwork and disputes between customers and sales teams.
  • Improve tracking, accountability, and reporting of deliveries.
  • Provide small dashboards that show POD completeness and pending signatures.

In this first version, the system is stand-alone. Orders are entered manually , and signed PODs are stored as digital records. Later, the concept can be extended to integrate with ERP systems such as SAP.
